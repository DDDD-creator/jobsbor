## 四、Hacker News "Show HN"

### 标题
Show HN: Jobsbor – Aggregated remote job board for Web3/Tech/FinTech

### 正文
I built a remote job aggregator that pulls from 14 sources (RemoteOK, Remotive, 
company APIs) and auto-updates every 6 hours. It also aggregates 300+ tech articles 
from RSS feeds (TechCrunch, Wired, CoinTelegraph, etc.) for a built-in blog.

Key features:
- 240+ jobs across Web3, FinTech, AI, Cloud
- 200 SEO-optimized industry × location landing pages
- Multi-language (EN/ZH)
- Auto-updating blog with 14 RSS sources

Built with Next.js, deployed on Vercel: https://jobsbor.vercel.app
